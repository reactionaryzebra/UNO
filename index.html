Rules

The goal of UNO is to play all of the cards in your hand. On each turn you have to play a card from your hand that matches the card in play either in color or number.

There are a few special cards:

  • Reverse: Reverses the order of play
  • Skip: Skips the next player's turn
  • Draw 2: The next player is forced to draw 2 cards
  • Wild: This card can be played at any time. When it's played you choose a color for the wild to represent
  • Wild Draw 4: Same functionas a Wild and also forces the next player to draw 4 cards

If you are unable to play a card from your hand, you must draw a card. If that card is playable, you may play it. Otherwise it is the next player's turn
